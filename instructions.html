Instructions

A few things you must remember before beginning your session.

Important: Perform gestures naturally, as you normally use your phone. Do not try to alter or fake gestures.

Gesture Guide

Task 1 — TAP
Tapping Single will start the gesture look and feel immediately.
Task 2 — SWIPE
Slide your finger across any direction. Left, right, up, or down gestures all count.
Task 3 — SCROLL
Drag your finger through the data, as if using a natural scrolling motion. A slight change will register each scroll.
Task 4 — ZOOM
Perform pinch-to-zoom using two fingers. Zoom in or out within the highlighted interaction zone.

Important Notes

1
Tapping Begin will start immediately. Data is captured for each gesture.
2
Data is guaranteed private — no personal identifiers are stored at any time.
3
Stop any time by pressing the Stop button. You can resume or cancel.
4
10 repetitions per gesture. 4 gestures per session × 3 sessions required.
