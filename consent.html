Informed Consent

Each user must undergo and answer the informed consent below as it is mandatory before proceeding with the study.

INFORMED CONSENT FORM

Purpose & Procedures
This study aims to collect multimodal touch attributes from a short series of touch interactions on mobile devices. You will be asked to perform each step as instructed on the screen.

Privacy & Identification

The app will not track attributes that identify you personally — no name, email, device ID, or exact location. Data is collected solely for biometric gesture research. All recordings are stored securely and will not be shared with third parties.

Data Collection

The app will record the touch attribute of each step you perform. You may be asked to repeat the same steps across multiple sessions. Session data includes timestamps and touch coordinates only.

Voluntary Participation

This study is completely voluntary. You may withdraw at any time without consequence. Your participation or non-participation will not affect your standing or academic record at this institution.

Risks & Benefits

There are no known risks beyond normal smartphone use. Your data will contribute to advancing touch-based biometric research.
